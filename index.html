About Company

Infreyes Private Limited started its operations with the vision a mission of creating solutions for medical diagnostics using non-invasive imaging. The company currently has a product which is in market-ready stage and couple of more in the pipeline.

Veinus

Veinus is an easy to use, portable, non-invasive, vein detecting device which visually assists in finding best vein for cannulation with no risks or side effects. It is especially useful for detecting subcutaneous veins which are not visible to naked eyes.
